Sustainability Companion Settings

Introduction

This page introduces all of the metrics that are used along with the associated weightage that is used for scoring.

Individual scores are ranked over 10, where 1 is worst score, 10 is best score. The total for each section is rebased over 100.

Environmental Score

  • Greenhouse Gas Emissions [35%] Total GHG Emissions
  • Material Sustainability [15%] How sustainable each material is
  • Water Usage [10%] Total amount of water used to produce the product
  • Packaging Impact [20%] Total sustainability of the packaging
  • End of Life Disposal [20%] How the product is disposed

Social Score

  • Fair Labour [10%] How many workers earn living wage
  • Worker Safety [10%] Number of Incidents / Health Policies
  • Fair Trade [20%] Is product certified
  • Local Sourcing [20%] Amount of Materials sourced locally
  • Community Impact [20%] Number of Donations / community projects per product
  • User Health and Safety [20%] Is Product free of harmful side effects

Governance Score

  • Affordability / Value [20%] Cost per use / cost over lifespan
  • Circular Economy Fit [25%] % of product/materials reusable/remanufacturable
  • Local Economic Impact [30%] Local job creation or SME partnerships
  • Supply Chain Resilience [15%] Supplier diversification
  • Innovation / R&D [10%] Investment in sustainable design or tech
